FAQ

Partner program — Rules
+

Straight answers—the kind we’d want before putting our own name on a product.

Please read these conditions before promoting PitchCV.

  • Reach $350.00 to unlock payouts. The minimum payout threshold is $350; payouts are manual and processed after approval.
  • No self-referrals. Self-referrals and coupon/deal-site traffic are prohibited.
  • 30% applies to the first paid monthly subscription charge only (after any 7-day trial). Trialing / trial signup fees do not count.
  • A one-time $20 welcome credit from PitchCV is added when you join the partner program (it counts toward your payout balance).
  • The attribution window is 30 days from a valid referral click.
  • No commission for registration, trial period, or email signup alone.
  • One commission per invited user; duplicates are rejected.
How do I track my referrals and earnings?
+
You'll get access to a partner dashboard where you can see clicks, sign-ups, conversions, and commission progress in real-time.
When does commission start accruing?
+
Commission is earned on the referred user’s first paid monthly subscription charge after any trial—not on registration, email signup, or trial activity alone. See Rules for the exact definition.
How long does the cookie last?
+
Our attribution window is 30 days. If someone clicks your link and subscribes within 30 days, you get the commission.
Why is the payout threshold $350?
+
This threshold protects both partners and the platform from fraud and micro-transaction overhead.
Can I use my link in paid ads?
+
Yes, but reach out first to ensure brand consistency and avoid conflicts with our own marketing.
What if a referred user cancels?
+
The program pays on a defined qualifying charge (see Rules), not on “lifetime subscription revenue.” Anything already credited and approved per the rules stays yours; there’s no stack of mystery future months to track.
Can I promote PitchCV outside the US?
+
Yes—many partners have global audiences. Your link, cookie window, and dashboard work the same; candidates join where PitchCV is available to them. When in doubt, say so honestly in your content—that’s the positioning we want.
